Speculative Pricing Buffer

Internal tool. Not linked from the public site. It sets a retail price that still hits the target margin after an expected acclimation loss on a tissue culture batch.

Formula: Final = (Base import cost × Markup) / (1 - Loss buffer). If the buffer is 20%, the surviving 80% of a batch carries the cost of the 20% that does not make it.
