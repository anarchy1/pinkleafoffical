## Key reads (size matters, this is the big caveat)
- Their pot 9 (large) plants fetch 1,200 (Maharani, Sanderiana Pink). Our TC/small
  prices are far below that. Signal: grown-out specimens can be priced far above
  our TC level. Do not compare a TC plantlet to a pot 9 plant.
- Dragon Scale: market sits at 150 (mint) to 350 (albo). Our April price was 420
  for both, so we are above market on Dragon Scale unless ours are larger.
- Sarian Albo, Simpo, Black Velvet: we were cheaper than this competitor. Room to
  raise toward their 450 to 500 if our size is comparable.
- Many of our varieties (Devil Monster, Venom, Cuprea series, Chantrieri,
  Watsoniana, Yucatan Princess, Azlanii) do NOT appear in this competitor's list.
  Rare = we set the price, no undercut pressure.
